!!! Look at the tutorial for cheatsheet of common formats:

    - Anchors: ^ and $
    - Quantifiers: * + ? and {}
    - OR operator: | or []
    - Character classes: \d \w \s and .
    - Flags: g/m/i
    - Grouping and capturing: ()
    - Bracket expressions: []
    - Greedy and Lazy match
    - Boundaries: \b and \B
    - Look-ahead and Look-behind:  (?=) and (?<=)
